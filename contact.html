Frequently asked questions

Are the score predictions accurate?
Our calculators use real College Board composite scoring weights and published 2024 exam data. They're close approximations — the College Board doesn't publish exact raw-to-scaled conversion tables, so there's a small margin of uncertainty. Think of it as a reliable estimate, not a guarantee.
Is CramLab really free?
Yes — all score calculators are free and will always be free. We may add an optional premium tier for practice tests in the future, but the core tools stay free.
When are practice tests coming?
Practice tests are our top priority after the calculator launch. Create a free account and you'll be among the first to know when they go live.
Can you add [subject]?
Probably! Use the form above to request a subject — requests directly influence what we build next.
Is CramLab affiliated with the College Board?
No. CramLab is an independent student tool. "AP" and "Advanced Placement" are registered trademarks of the College Board, which does not endorse this site.
