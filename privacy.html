Privacy Policy

We keep this simple. Here's exactly what we collect, why, and what we do with it.

Last updated: January 2025

CramLab is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you use CramLab, how we use it, and your rights around it.

1. What we collect

Without an account: We collect nothing. No cookies, no tracking, no stored data.

With an account: We collect your name, email address, and calculator scores (so we can save your progress). We also collect basic usage data (which subjects you use) so we can improve the site.

Google sign-in: We receive your Google display name and email only. We never receive your password or any other Google account data.

2. How we use your data

  • To save your calculator scores so you can pick up where you left off
  • To understand which subjects are used most, so we can prioritize what to build next
  • To send account emails (password reset, etc.) — we do not send marketing emails

We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers or third parties for marketing.

3. How we store your data

Your account and scores are stored securely using Google Firebase. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Firebase security rules ensure only you can read or write your own data.

4. Advertising

CramLab is currently ad-free. When we launch publicly, we may display ads through Google AdSense. If we do, Google may use cookies to show relevant ads. You can opt out at adssettings.google.com. We will update this policy before any advertising is enabled.

5. Your rights

  • Access: View all your data in Account settings
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all data from the Account page — permanent and immediate
  • Correction: Update your name and email in Account settings

6. Children's privacy

CramLab is designed for high school students. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us and we'll delete it promptly.

7. Contact

Questions? Contact us and we'll respond promptly.
