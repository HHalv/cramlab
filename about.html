Why we built this

AP exams are high-stakes. A 4 or 5 can mean thousands of dollars in college credit saved — but knowing how close you are to that score has always been a guessing game. Tools that actually help are locked behind $30/month paywalls.

CramLab flips that. We built accurate score calculators using real College Board composite scoring weights, combined with 2024 national distribution data so you can see exactly where you stand. All of it free, no account required.

We're a small team building fast — the core calculator works right now for any student who needs it, and practice tests are next.
